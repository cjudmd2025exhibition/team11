WHERE CAN I WATCH A GAME?

Explore where to enjoy Korean Baseball! Each stadium offers a unique vibe. From bustling Seoul to charming smaller cities, find the perfect spot to watch. Check each stadium's seating, facilities, and atmosphere to pick the best place for you.

  • Seoul
  • Suwon
  • Incheon
  • Daejeon
  • Busan
  • Changwon
  • Gwangju
  • Daegu
01

Seoul Sports Complex Baseball Stadium

It is used as the home ground for the KBO League's Doosan Bears and LG Twins, and is commonly referred to as Jamsil Baseball Stadium.

19-2, Songpa-gu, Seoul Sports Complex
Teams: Doosan Bears, LG Twins
02

Suwon KT Wiz Park

Home to the KT Wiz, this modern stadium in Suwon opened in 2015. It features cutting-edge facilities and offers excellent sightlines from every seat.

1 Suwon-ro, Paldal-gu, Suwon
Team: KT Wiz
03

Incheon SSG Landers Field

The SSG Landers call this beautiful waterfront stadium home. Located in Incheon, it offers stunning views of the surrounding area and is known for great food options.

618 Munemi-ro, Michuhol-gu, Incheon
Team: SSG Landers
04

Daejeon Hanwha Life Ballpark

Home of the Hanwha Eagles, this stadium in central South Korea creates an intimate atmosphere where fans feel close to the action.

42 Daejong-ro, Jung-gu, Daejeon
Team: Hanwha Eagles
05

Sajik Baseball Stadium

Located in Busan, home to the Lotte Giants. Known for having some of the most passionate fans in Korean baseball, the atmosphere here is truly electric.

45 Sajik-ro, Dongnae-gu, Busan
Team: Lotte Giants
06

Changwon NC Park

The NC Dinos play at this modern facility in Changwon. It features comfortable seating and excellent amenities for a premium baseball experience.

361 Changwon-ro, Changwon
Team: NC Dinos
07

Gwangju-KIA Champions Field

Home of the KIA Tigers, one of the most successful teams in Korea. Located in Gwangju, this stadium offers a traditional baseball experience with dedicated fans.

1 Imbangul-ro, Buk-gu, Gwangju
Team: KIA Tigers
08

Daegu Samsung Lions Park

Home to the Samsung Lions in Daegu. Famous for its passionate crowd and excellent facilities, it offers an unforgettable baseball experience.

125 Jangdeung-ro, Suseong-gu, Daegu
Team: Samsung Lions
